# Employee Benefits FAQ

## Health insurance
Al-Riyadh Holdings offers medical, dental, and vision through Bupa Arabia. Coverage starts on day 1. Employees pay 20% of the premium; Al-Riyadh covers the rest.

## Parental leave
All employees are eligible for 16 weeks of paid parental leave after 6 months of tenure. This applies equally to birthing and non-birthing parents.

## Retirement
401(k) with 5% company match, vesting over 3 years. Administered by Fidelity.

## Paid time off
Unlimited PTO with a 15-day annual minimum. Sabbatical of 4 weeks available after 5 years of service.

## Wellness stipend
$1,200 annual stipend for gym, therapy, or wellness apps. Submit receipts via Expensify.
